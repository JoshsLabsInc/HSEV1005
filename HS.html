The Action Plan

To avoid critical tipping points, we must triple renewable energy production by 2030.

1. Solar & Wind Surge

Deploy 1.5 TW annually.

Incentivize rooftop solar, offshore wind farms, and community projects. Cost reduction of 60% by 2028.

2. Smart Grids + Storage

AI-managed grids & batteries.

Eliminate curtailment, enable 100% renewable integration with next-gen lithium‑sulfur batteries.

3. Global Energy Equity Fund

Redirect fossil subsidies.

Finance decentralized renewables for 800 million people, creating energy democracy.

4. Circular Economy Mandate

100% recyclable components.

Zero‑waste solar panels and turbine blades by 2028, rare earth recycling infrastructure.

5. Electrification of Transport

100% zero‑emission vehicle sales by 2035.

Expand high‑speed rail, urban EV fleets, and charging corridors powered entirely by renewables.

6. Nature‑Based Resilience

Restore ecosystems as carbon sinks.

Reforestation, soil carbon sequestration, and community microgrids that adapt to climate extremes.
